About Us

Fifteen years of building things people actually use.

Raga Designers started in Chennai as a small team of designers and developers who believed a website's job is to work quietly and well — not just look good in a screenshot. Today the same studio builds and maintains sites, storefronts and apps for clients across India and beyond, without losing the hands-on process that got us here.

Design and development sit in the same room. Every project moves through one team, from wireframe to the server it ships on, so nothing gets lost in translation along the way.
